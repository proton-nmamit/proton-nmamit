Our Mission

PROTON is the official cybersecurity association of our college, dedicated to creating a security-aware community. We focus on bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications through engaging workshops, hands-on training, and cybersecurity competitions.

At PROTON, we believe in learning through experience, encouraging collaboration, and staying ahead in the ever-evolving world of cybersecurity. Whether you're a beginner exploring the field or an expert honing your skills, our community provides the perfect platform to grow, connect, and innovate in the realm of cybersecurity.

Welcome to Proton Club. Think deeper. Hack smarter. Stay secure.
